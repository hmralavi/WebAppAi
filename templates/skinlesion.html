Skin Lesion Classification

This Ai application can detect 7 types of skin lesions. Simply, upload an image and get the result.
Please check out the provided examples to see the supported types of the skin lesions.
The model is trained on HAM10000 Dataset.

Disclaimer: This skin lesion classifier model is intended for educational and demonstrative purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
